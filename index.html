Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

SOPNEZ SL ("we", "our", or "us") operates an affiliate management platform that integrates with TikTok Shop and other third-party services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information through our platform:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • TikTok Shop Data: When you connect your TikTok Shop account via OAuth, we access affiliate/creator information, order data, commission details, and product information as authorized by you.
  • Affiliate Data: TikTok handles, Discord user IDs, funnel stage, video performance metrics (views, likes, shares), and engagement data.
  • Usage Data: Log data, browser type, and interaction data with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our affiliate management services.
  • To sync affiliate and order data from TikTok Shop on your behalf.
  • To track video performance and engagement metrics.
  • To automate follow-up communications via Discord.
  • To generate reports on affiliate performance, GMV, and commissions.
  • To improve our platform and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure located in the United States:

  • Database: PostgreSQL hosted on Neon, with AES-256 encryption at rest.
  • Application: Hosted on Vercel with TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit.
  • Background Services: Hosted on Railway/Fly.io with encrypted connections.

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), secure credential storage via environment variables, role-based access controls, and the principle of least privilege for data access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting providers (Neon, Vercel, Railway, Upstash) that process data on our behalf.
  • TikTok Shop: Data transmitted back to TikTok Shop as part of the integration functionality you authorized.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your active account and business relationship. Upon termination of services:

  • We will delete all personal data and TikTok Shop data within 30 days of your request.
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Disconnect your TikTok Shop account at any time through our platform settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. TikTok Shop Integration

When you connect your TikTok Shop account:

  • We access only the data scopes you explicitly authorize during the OAuth flow.
  • Access tokens are stored encrypted and refreshed automatically.
  • You can revoke access at any time through TikTok Shop settings or our platform.
  • Upon revocation, we delete all associated TikTok Shop tokens and cease data collection.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach involving personal data, we will:

  • Notify affected users and TikTok Shop within 72 hours of discovery.
  • Notify relevant regulatory authorities as required by applicable law.
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach.

10. International Data Transfers

Data is primarily stored and processed in the United States. If you are located outside the US, by using our services you consent to the transfer of your data to the US, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
